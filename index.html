Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?▼
Yes, Resume Builder Pro is 100% free. No hidden charges, no credit card required. Unlimited PDF downloads and unlimited resumes.
Can I download my resume as PDF?▼
Absolutely! One-click PDF export with high-quality print resolution. You can also print directly from your browser.
Is login required?▼
No login is required. All your data is stored locally in your browser. You can also export a JSON backup anytime.
Is it ATS friendly?▼
Yes! All templates are designed to be ATS-compatible. You can also enable ATS mode to strip colors and images for maximum compatibility.
Can I create multiple resumes?▼
Yes, you can create unlimited resumes. Use the "My Resumes" manager to organize, duplicate, and archive them.
